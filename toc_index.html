Biographical Information

Raised in the Middle West, Seth Frame has over a decade of experience in GIS; most of that time hes been spent in cultural resource management. In a previous life he perfomed GIS services for archaeological companies in the intermountain West, he now lives in New York where he is employed as a GIS Analyst for a software development company. Seth recently graduated from Colorado State University’s online anthropology program in Decenber of 2016 and is Currently enrolled in teh University of Wisconsin-Madison's GIS and Web Mapping Masters program.
