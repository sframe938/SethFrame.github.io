Santa Fe Trail Bicycle Trek Map

A map detailing the route of the 2016 Santa Fe Bicycle Trek. The route stretches from Santa Fe, NM to New Franklin, MO and coveres approximatly 1096 miles. The trek lasted a total of twenty days with breaks every five days. 2016 was the last year for the bicycle trek which was in its twenty-second year.

National Park Service Visitation Map

A web map illustrating NPS visitation over a ten year period, from 2007 to 2016. The different national park service properties are broken down by type. The user can view each property by type through the 10 year period, all the properties, or just the top 20 most visited. The default view is of the most visited properties.

Language Usage in the Southwest

An interactive map and chart detailing the language usage across the Four-Corners area of the Southwestern United States. Language usage is shown at the county level and depicts the use of English, Spanish, Navajo, and other Native American languages. Percent of language use is shown by county across the states of Colorado, New Mexico, Arizona, and Utah. Reservation boundaries can be added to the map for reference as well.

Great Lakes Watercolor Style Map

A little cartographic fun. This map was produced using ArcPro with map styles downloaded from here:
Steal this Watercolor Style for Pro, Please
Represented on the map are the five great lakes: Huron, Ontario, Michigan, Erie, and Superior. Additionally, minor waterways and lakes are also present, along with urban areas and state and national forest areas. Growing up in the Midwest, the Great Lakes have always held an attraction for me. This map combines my love of those lakes with my interests in watercolor and cartography.
